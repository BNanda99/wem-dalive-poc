## Code of Conduct

### A message from our Chief Executive Officer

At PhonePe, we believe that ethical business practices are fundamental to our success and the trust our customers place in us. Our commitment to integrity, transparency, and responsible conduct guides every decision we make and every action we take.

This Code of Conduct serves as our compass, helping us navigate the complex landscape of modern business while staying true to our values. It outlines the standards we expect from every member of our team and reflects our dedication to creating a positive impact in the communities we serve.

We encourage all employees to familiarize themselves with these guidelines and to reach out with any questions or concerns. Together, we can continue to build a company that not only delivers exceptional financial services but also upholds the highest ethical standards.
